Sudbury's Premier Kennel-Free Boarding Facility

Lori Johnston, owner of Cachou & Company, started her journey into a pet business after a lifetime of being mentored by her aunt who actively rescued & rehomed cats in Sudbury throughout the 70s and 80s, volunteering with local rescue Pet Save Sudbury and after working as a veterinary assistant in 2018 and 2019.

After walking away from her most recent careers in retail and fast-food management in 2020 to become primary caregiver for both of her parents, Lori found herself looking for a new, sustainable future doing something that she could fully give her heart and soul to.

With her lifelong love of pets, experience caring for them, and volunteering in the community to help displaced, abandoned or injured rescue dogs and cats, Lori launched her pet boarding business with a dream of building a pet resort complete with fenced in play areas, a pond, trail system, training facility and grooming salon â€“ a place where pet owners can access all of their pet care needs and feel completely at ease that their beloved pets would be cared for with the utmost love and attention.

Lori launched Cachou & Company in December of 2020 in a temporary location while she secured and renovated an appropriately zoned rural property to build her dream. She hosts both cats and dogs, cage-free and kennel-free for both daycare and boarding in an open concept facility. Located just east of Sudbury, near Wahnapitae, Lori welcomes pets to come play, enjoy the outdoors and be social with others. She ensures plenty of exercise, a calm, quiet, safe environment and all the love and attention your pet deserves.
